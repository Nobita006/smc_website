Privacy Statement

Effective Date: 2/09/2024

1. Overview

Welcome to Social Media Campaigns Ltd. This privacy statement outlines how we handle, use, and safeguard the personal data you provide when accessing our website and its services.

2. Data We Gather

We collect different types of personal information that you provide to us directly, such as when you sign up on our platform, show interest in our services, engage in activities on the website, or reach out to us directly.

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email address, and contact details.
  • Usage Information: Comprises IP addresses, browser types, and pages you visit.
  • Cookie Data: Utilizes session cookies, preference cookies, and security cookies.

3. Purpose of Data Usage

We use the data we collect or receive for various purposes, including:

  • To deliver and manage our services efficiently.
  • To communicate administrative information to you.
  • To secure our services and enforce our legal terms and policies.
  • To respond to lawful requests and mitigate potential risks.

4. Information Sharing Practices

We may share your personal information in certain circumstances, such as:

  • With Third-Party Partners: We may share your data with third-party vendors, contractors, and agents who provide services on our behalf.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your data when it is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect the safety of users or others, address fraud concerns, or respond to government inquiries.

5. Data Security Measures

We employ various administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to secure your personal data. However, please be aware that no method of online transmission or storage is entirely foolproof.

6. Your Privacy Rights

As a user, you are entitled to several rights regarding your personal data, such as:

  • Right to Access: You are entitled to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You have the right to request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data under specific circumstances.

7. Policy Modifications

We may revise this privacy statement periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page to keep you informed of how we protect your data.

Essential Advice for Parents to Guide Teens Online

  • Establish clear rules and guidelines for using social media, balancing freedom with safety.
  • Educate them about the dangers of oversharing and the importance of protecting personal details.
  • Stay engaged with their digital lives by regularly discussing what they post and follow.
  • Help them develop critical thinking skills to evaluate the reliability of online content and sources.
